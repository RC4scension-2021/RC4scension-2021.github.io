Thoth

Known as the god of writing, magic, wisdom, and the moon, the people under Thoth are wise but more importantly, willingly shares their wisdom with others. They are fair and level-headed, able to make think on their feet to win any confrontation.


Osiris

The God of the Deceased, and resilience exemplified, people of Osiris are strong and able to withstand the toughest of tribulations. They do not hold resentment and foster harmony, but do not mistaken it for weakness for Osiris is the judge of the dead who oversaw one's life on earth.


Neith

The war goddess and goddess of creation, Neith can as easily take a life as she can create it. The people who follow her are determined, skilled and focused. They will stop at nothing till they achieve their goals. Armed with the mental fortitude to take on any challenge, others should think twice confronting followers of Neith.


Sekhmet

Seen as the protector of the pharaohs and led them in warfare, people of Sekhmet are fearless, tough and hungry for success. With the title "One Before Whom Evil Trembles", they are the kings and queens of the battlefield and will make those who stand in their way regret their decision.


Horus

God of the sky, restorer of order, and the avenger of wrongs, the Horus people have strong moral compasses and stands up for what is right. They are brave protectors who stands up for the weak and vulnerable, embodying the values of the benevolent god.
