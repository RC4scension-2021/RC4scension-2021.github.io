Ready to expose the killer?

You and your team have 1 hour to break into the killer's laptop and expose his identity! The whole country is counting on you!

RULES

  • - 1 to 5 players
  • - PC/laptop is necessary, preferably 1 per player
  • - Google is allowed and even recommended!
  • - Communication is very important, even if you play via video chat
  • - Try to all start the game at the same time to synchronize the timer for everyone.
  • - Cheating (e.g.: dev tools) is not allowed, everything can be solved
  • - If you're stuck anyway, try to consult a tip
  • - Preferably use Google Chrome or Firefox, with the browser at full screen size
  • - Do not refresh your browser, because that will reset the timer and your progress will be lost
  • - Stuck? press here for extra tips and solutions!
