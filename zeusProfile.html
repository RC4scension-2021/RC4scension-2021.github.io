Kronos

Known as the god of time, in particular time when viewed as a destructive, all-devouring force. He ruled the cosmos during the Golden Age. The people of Kronos are known for their excellent time management and impeccable leadership skills. No one dares to defy the King of the Titans.


Kratos

Known as the divine personification of strength, the people of Kratos are known to be merciless and brutal on the battlefield. With their only goal to protect the rule of Zeus, the people of Kratos will track down anyone who dares to challenge it and make them pay for their decision.


Hermes

Known as the God of trade, luck and thieves, Herald of the Gods, and regarded as the divine trickster, the people of Hermes are always able to use their cunning nature to turn the tide of any battle against anyone who dares to challenge them.


Chiron

Unlike other centaurs, who were notorious for being wild, violent and generally uncultured, Chiron was known for his intelligence and kindness. Known to be the wisest and justest of all the centaurs, the people of Chiron will use their breadth of knowledge to aid anyone who comes to them for help.


Artemis

Known as the Goddess of the hunt, the people of Artemis are patient, yet deadly. Those who seek to challenge the people of Artemis will find themselves outwitted at every turn, while those who try to escape will only find themselves trapped in the keen eye of the Artemis people.
