Ixchel

Being the goddess of the moon, medicine and love, Ixchel is known for her wisdom and benevolence for the world. She perceives the power to create and restore life, yet to destroy life as well. The people of Ixchel are affectionate and kind towards all beings on earth but will not hesitate to fight against any injustice and shatter all the evilness.


Kinich Ahau

Known as the God of the sun and believed to turn into a jaguar at night, Kinich Ahau is representative of power, ferocity, and valor. The people of Kinich Ahau are warriors of courage and intelligence. They are not typically aggressive, but when they becomes aggressive, it's with explosive force.


Chaac

Being the God of rain, lightning, and storms, Chaac possesses the power to summon the powers of nature to his will. He is essential for the survival of the civilisation which also grants him the ability to easily wipe out the civilisation. The people of Chaac are fearless, calm and grow even more powerful in the midst of chaos.


Yum Kaax

Known as the God of the forest, Yum Kaax is the protector of the jungle and guardian of wild plants and animals. He is invoked by hunters who depend upon his goodwill. The people of Yum Kaax are hunters who are confident and patient with deadly killer instincts and tenacious viability.


Huraqan

Known as the God of wind, storm and fire, Huraqan is able to command elements of the Earth while keeping his people away from the damage of natural abominations. The people of Huraqan are deemed to be immiscible and immune to the invasions of other tribes, as they always have the favour of the nature under the protection of Huraqan.
