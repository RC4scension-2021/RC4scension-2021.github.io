Skadi

Being the Goddess of winter, the people of Skadi symbolizes darkness and cold, yet is the most benevolent of the Odin clan. Passionate in pursuit of adventure and justice, thier free-spiritedness brings them through difficult terrains and weathers, navigating through them with the help of their wolf Kaldr. The people of Skadi embodies strength, courage and endurance but most importantly prudence in all matters. Despite their independence, Skadi’s loyalty will always rest with the Odin clan.


Loki

Gifted with extraordinary intellect, people of Loki are known for their mischief and expert strategic skills in battle. As the god of fire, Loki's powerful and unpredictable nature is likened to that of a flame to many enemies. Through their cunning and wit, they aim to make the other clans realize that Odin is more than what it seems.


Thor

As the God of Thunder, we the descendants of Thor are trained to reign as one. With limitless strength and trusty Mjolnir, we are not only granted mastery over elements of thunder and lighting but also, gifted with limitless strength to rise against all odds. Calling for the calculative, strong and loyal, trust is only accorded to those who are worthy. Behold Thor OG - the son of gods, we have no limits we cannot test.


Heimdall

Blessed with Omni-senses, in particular the ability to see all that is happening within the Nine Realms, the people of Heimdall are the gatekeepers of the Bifrost and work tirelessly to keep Asgard safe from all threats physical or magical. Heimdall is also equipped with Gjallerhorn, his magic horn which helps to rally all of Asgard in times of crisis. Heimdall is loyal to a fault to the throne and places his allegiance to his people above all else. Heimdall's power and fervor for their roles as gatekeepers has defended Odin from other clans for countless winters.


Sol

As descendants of the Sun Goddess, they are renowned for their supportive and warm nature, akin to a knight in shining armour. Though radiant as they can be, never get on their bad side, as they would burn and chase you in their chariots till the end of time.
