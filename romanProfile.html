Vulcan

Vulcan is the god of fire and destruction, but also of metalworking and craftsmen. As the aspect of fire, he represented both its destructive and fertilising powers. We as Vulcan will burn all who stand before us, unyielding against all odds and none will stand before us but in doing so we build up our own, fostering creativity and growth.
